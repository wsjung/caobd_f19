'''Smokescreen''' is a [[fictional character]] that appeared in [[comic books]] published by [[Marvel Comics]].  Smokescreen is a criminal looking to take over the illegal gambling business. He appears in the public service comic ''Spider-Man Storm and Power Man Battle Smokescreen'' and was created to make readers aware of the dangers of smoking. He momentarily captures [[Storm (Marvel Comics)|Storm]] then is captured by [[Spider-Man]] with the assistance of [[Power Man]] who then hand him over into police custody.
