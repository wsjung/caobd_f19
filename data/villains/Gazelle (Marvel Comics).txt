'''Gazelle''' is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].

==Publication history==
Gazelle first appeared in ''[[Fantastic Four]]'' vol. 1 #186 (September 1977), and was created by [[Len Wein]] and [[George Pérez]].

The character subsequently appears in ''Fantastic Four Annual'' #14 (1979), ''Fantastic Four'' #223 (October 1980), ''The Vision and the Scarlet Witch'' #3 (December 1985), ''The Avengers 2000 Annual'', ''Marvel Knights: 4'' #25-27 (February–April 2006), and ''Four'' #30 (August 2006).

Gazelle appeared as part of the "Salem's Seven" entry in the ''[[Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe|Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe Deluxe Edition]]'' #19.

==Fictional character biography==
Gazelle is a daughter of [[Nicholas Scratch]] and is  a member of [[Salem's Seven]].

[[Wizard (Marvel Comics)|Wizard]] reformed the [[Frightful Four]] using Gazelle, [[Reptilla]], and [[Vertigo (Salem's Seven)|Vertigo]] of [[Salem's Seven]], and they attacked Chicago to get the attention of [[Mister Fantastic]]. Mister Fantastic was almost defeated by the Frightful Four until [[Scarlet Witch]] appeared to help him.<ref>''[[Fantastic Four]]'' Vol. 5 #10</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Gazelle has super-speed and agility.
